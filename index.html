How to play:

Buy factories and progress until you can buy the golden potato.

On every factory start off by manually working and go on to buying workers to make your factory more productive.

The final stage should always be to make it auto-work for you.

The Mine has no requirements to run, but all others need something from the previous factory to run.

Good Luck and Have Fun!
